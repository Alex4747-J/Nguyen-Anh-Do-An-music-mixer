What is Valorant?

Welcome to Valorant, a free-to-play, 5v5 tactical first-person shooter where sharp gunplay meets powerful character abilities. You'll play as an "Agent," each equipped with a unique set of skills designed for strategic advantage. In the main game mode, one team attacks by trying to plant a bomb called the Spike, while the other team defends. Success demands more than just aim; it requires teamwork, clever use of your abilities, and sharp strategy to outplay your opponents. Choose your Agent, coordinate with your team, and fight to win 13 rounds to claim victory.
